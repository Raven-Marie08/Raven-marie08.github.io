The Weeknd is a grammy-winning R&B singer.He is one of the leading performers in the music industry for alternative R&B.He has made top-selling albums like "Can't feel my face," "The Hills", and "Call out my name." Orginally from Toronto,Canada The Weeknd drop out of high school to further his musical career. Which led him to work with musical artists like Drake.

In 2012 The Weeknd released his record "Trilogy" which became a platinum-selling record.In 2015 "Beauty Behind the Madness" became the Number 1 album. Along with the success of this album came seven nominations for the 2016 Grammy Awards, winning for Best R&B Performance and Best Urban Contemporary Album.The Weeknd contintued to ride to the top with his 2016 release "Starboy" feturing other artist like Kendrick Lamar and Lana Del Rey.In 2018 he relesed yet another successful album "My Dear Melancholy" feturing the popular hit "Call Out My Name."

In 2019 The Weeknd made his film debut alongside Adam Sandler in "Uncut Gems." He has also been a musical guest on the comedy show "SNL." Appearing for a total of three times in 2015,2016,and in 2020. The Weeknd continued on to drop his singles "Blinding Lights" and "Heartless," before releasing his 2020 album "After Hours which is still number 1 on the Billboard 200 chart for the fourth week in a row." The Weeknd is obviously a huge success and millions of people have listened to his music and really like him as an artist.
